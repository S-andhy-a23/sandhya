Awareness

How to react when encountered these tactics

Lying

Call out lies when you catch them and stop assuming that they’re telling you the truth. If you always expect they’re lying, you won’t fall into any traps.

Gaslighting

Stop second-guessing yourself. This can be hard to do if you’re already being gaslit, so consider leaving yourself notes or recording your behaviors

Confidence Breaching

Once someone shares a secret of yours, it’s a clear sign you should no longer tell them any secrets. Just don’t tell them anything personal going forward.
