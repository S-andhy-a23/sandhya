Awareness

How to react when encountered these tactics

Love Flooding

Dismiss any requests with kindness, but do it quickly and move on. If they stop being nice to you, you know they’re love bombing you.

Love Withholding

Don’t take the cold shoulder personally. Do your best to ignore any love withholding. They obviously need you more than you need them.

Choice Restriction

Don’t accept the false options presented to you. The world is rarely so straightforward that there are only 2-3 options, so if someone tells you you’re backed into choosing something, reject the premise.
